Welcome to MSIC

Here at MSIC, The Manchester Self-Improvement Club our goal is to help people on there journey of self improvment. We provide guidence such a tips and tricks on how to improve oneself, not limited to but including. Different yoga routines targeting different levels of difficulty and muscle groups, some excersise to help learn and expand your mindfulness and finally different methods of meditation. We understand that everyone is at there own level so we provide a more personalised schedule to follow.


Meditation

Meditation is a well know method of self improvement and its for a good reason. Meditation helps by stopping your mind from wondering or overthinking about irrelavent things, people often say It can help reduce stress and improve focus. It can also help you recharge or unwine after a long day.

Yoga

Yoga is a way of understanding your body and its limits. Its helps you have a better understanding of what youu can and cant do, it also sets a baseline for when you're doing an activity that pushes you more than normal. It can also help improve flexibility reduce joint and muscle pain and help your mental wellbeing too.

Mindfulness

Mindfulness is a type of meditation but it kind of different in a way. Meditation focuses more on reeling in your thoughts to calm your mind, however Mindfulness focuses on being more aware of what you're sensing and/or feeling in any given moment without interpretation or judgment. It's feeling your emotions but not being controlled by them.
