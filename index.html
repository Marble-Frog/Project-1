Welcome to MSIC

Here at MSIC, The Manchester Self-Improvement Club our goal is to help people on there journey of self improvment. We provide guidence such a tips and tricks on how to improve oneself, not limited to but including. Different yoga routines targeting different levels of difficulty and muscle groups, some excersise to help learn and expand your mindfulness and finally different methods of meditation. We understand that everyone is at there own level so we provide a more personalised schedule to follow.


Meditation

Meditation is a well know method of self improvement and its for a good reason. Meditation helps by stopping your mind from wondering or overthinking about something, people often say that they feel refreshed or recharged after meditating.
